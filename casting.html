CASTING


On 15th each month for year 2024 in the Palace of Culture and Sport, hall "Trakia".


Applicants must make a performance of their choice - sing a song, tell a story, play an instrument or dance.


Monthly fee 25 euros. Children up to six years old are free.


Find us at 31 Ivan Aksakov Str., Varna, Bulgaria. 0877081101
